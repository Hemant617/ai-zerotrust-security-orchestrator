‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) through Stripe. We also support PayPal for annual plans.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
The Free plan is available forever with no credit card required. You can upgrade to Pro or Enterprise anytime to unlock additional features.
What happens if I exceed my limits?
On the Free plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade when you reach your daily limit. Pro and Enterprise plans have unlimited scans, so you'll never hit a limit.
Do you offer discounts for annual billing?
Yes! Save 20% when you pay annually. Pro is $95.90/year (instead of $119.88) and Enterprise is $950/year (instead of $1,188).
Can I get a refund?
Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
